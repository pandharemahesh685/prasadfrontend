Position: position is used to keep an element at different places of screen.
There are 5 positions available in CSS.
i. static
ii. relative
iii. absolute
iv. fixed
v. sticky

i. static: static is the default position of every element. it will not have any impact of top,left,right or bottom

ii. relative: relative moves the element based on its previous place.It applies top,bottom,left and right

iii. absolute: absolute moves the element based on body . it applies top,left,right , bottom as of body.

iv. fixed: The Probelm with absolute is , it will scroll with page but fixed is nothing but absolute without any impact of scrolling

v. sticky: sticky is used to create element with relative and fixed properties. it becomes fixes after specific point
